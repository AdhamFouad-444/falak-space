Who We Are

Building the Infrastructure for Space Learning

Our Mission

FALAk exists to build structured, interactive learning environments for understanding space systems. We believe meaningful participation in space begins with clarity — not just information, but structured understanding.

Our goal is to create a layered learning pathway that moves from foundational concepts to applied systems thinking, using guided visualisation and controlled interaction. Long term, we aim to contribute to stronger internal space capability across the MENA region by helping develop technical understanding at scale.

Our Story

FALAk was founded by a group of University of Leeds students across Business, Physics with Astrophysics, and Mathematics. We recognised a gap between curiosity about space and structured understanding of how space systems actually work.

Most resources are either static and theoretical or highly technical and inaccessible. FALAk is designed to bridge that gap through carefully structured learning architecture (Framework 001), beginning with animations and guided interaction before progressing toward simulation. We are building FALAk as a long-term platform — not just content, but learning infrastructure.
